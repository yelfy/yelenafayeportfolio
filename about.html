Graphic Design, Illustrations
With a background in animation

As a graphic designer my work is deeply influenced by the art of animation, where every line has a purpose, and contributes to the story. I believe that design, much like animation, should be clear, intentional and fluid. My approach emphasizes readability and functionality, ensuring that each element enhaces the overall message. My aim is to make each interaction with my work both meaningful and memorable.
